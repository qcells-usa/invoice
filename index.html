Invoice Guidelines

Hanwha Q CELLS USA Corp.

Dear Vendors,

Thank you for working with Hanwha Q CELLS USA Corp. Please follow the below invoice guidelines to ensure swift payment processing for your work. All invoices must have the following information; otherwise, payment may be delayed while we work to clarify your submission. Please don't hesitate to reach out to usa-ap@qcells.com if you have any questions.

Invoice Requirements

  • Must contain a unique invoice number. Duplicate invoice numbers will be rejected.
  • Only clean, legible copies will be accepted. Illegible copies will be rejected.
  • For project-specific invoices, please ensure you have received a Purchase Order Number (PO) or PPRC number from our procurement team before invoice submission.
  • Invoices must match the Task Order/Purchase Order and/or the Payment Application/Schedule of Value.
  • Invoicing in excess of 100% of the line item will be rejected.
  • Invoices with material purchases must indicate items as taxable, nontaxable, or sales tax inclusive.
  • For general invoices, you may submit with "General" or "N/A" on the PO# line in the submission form.

Additional Documentation

If this is your first time working with us, please also provide the following:

  • Current year signed W-9 for tax purposes.
  • Current wire instructions/preferred payment method.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at usa-ap@qcells.com.
