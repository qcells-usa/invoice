Invoice Submission Guide

Dear Vendors,

Thank you for working with Hanwha Q CELLS USA Corp. Please follow the below invoice guidelines to ensure swift payment processing for your work. All invoices must have the following information, otherwise payment may be delayed while we work to clarify your work. Please don't hesitate to reach out to usa-ap@qcells.com if you have any questions.

  1. Must contain an unique invoice number. Duplicate invoice numbers will be rejected
  2. Only clean, legible copies will be accepted. Illegible copies will be rejected
  3. For project-specific invoices, please be sure you have received a Purchase Order Number (PO) or PPRC number from our procurement team before invoice submission
    • Invoices must match the Task Order/Purchase Order and/or the Payment Application/Schedule of Value
    • Invoicing in excess of 100% of the line item will be rejected
    • invoices with material purchases must indicate items as taxable, nontaxable, or sales tax inclusive
  4. For general invoices, you may submit with "General" or "N/A" on the PO# line in the submission form
  5. If this is your first time working with us, please also provide the following:
    • Current year signed W-9 for tax purpose
    • Current wire instructions/preferred payment method
